--- SECTION 1: RISK FACTORS ---
1. Supply Chain Concentration: We rely on a single-source foundry for 85% of our neural-processing units. Any disruption in East Asia would halt production.
2. Regulatory Compliance: Ongoing investigations by the European Commission regarding data sovereignty and privacy could lead to fines of up to 4% of global turnover.
3. Intangible Asset Impairment: Our valuation depends heavily on 452 pending patents. Legal challenges to our "Core-Sync" architecture represent a significant risk to market dominance.
